About the School

Football For Everyone

Our Saturday Morning Coaching School is the heart of FC Hanley. Every week our FA-qualified coaches deliver high-energy, fun sessions for children of all ages and abilities.

Whether your child is picking up a ball for the first time or looking to develop their game, there's a place for them here. Sessions are structured, safe, and most importantly — fun.
